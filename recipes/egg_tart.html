Description

This will be one of the best home-made pizza if it is made with the following ingredients and love.

Prep Time: 15mins
Cook Time: 10mins
Additional Time: 5mins
Total Time: 30mins
Servings: 6
Yield: 1 14-inch pizza

ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce)package prebaked pizza crust
  • 1 cup diced cooked chicken breast
  • 3 tbps Buffalo wing sauce
  • 1 (4 ounce) package crumbled blue cheeses
  • 1 stalk celery, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
